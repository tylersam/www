This company was founded on a mission to fight world hunger. For every meal purchased, a meal is donated through Feed My Starving Children. Each store displays a continuously updating meal count helping guests connect with the company mission. The marketing team also uses the system to showcase current initiatives and promotions.

The frontend of this project is an Apple TV App distributed through the company's MDM. I built the app using React Native with a Ruby on Rails backend. Apple TVs allow for simple company-wide management and offer a user-friendly experience for store leaders.

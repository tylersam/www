My wife and I built this tool to break down her Bible translation school assignments into (approximately) even tasks. It allows her to track her progress and redistribute work over available workdays if she gets behind. It also generates PDFs that she imports into GoodNotes for writing her translation assignments on her iPad. We're hoping to continue adding features and turn this into our household dashboard (habit tracking, calendar, weather, etc.).
